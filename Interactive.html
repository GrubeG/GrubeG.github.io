Interactive Maps

Indiana Jones Promo Basemap

Designed for my Graphic Design in Cartography class at UW-Madison using Mapbox Studio, this is a tileset designed to promote a hypothetical Indiana Jones movie using the style of the movies as inspiration.

City of Janesville Webmap

Designed when I worked for the City of Janesville, this was created to replace the existing multi-purpose webmap. With many layers to activate and multiple search capabilities, it was a major undertaking to design.

Chippewa County Frac Sand Mining

Designed when I was interning at the UW Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering using Tilemill and Leaflet, this webmap shows the Frac Sand mining locations and surrounding areas in Chippewa County, WI.
