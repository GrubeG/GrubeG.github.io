About Me

Hello, I'm Greg and thanks again for looking at my site! I've been a GIS professional since 2009, when I discovered that making maps doesn't mean you have to be good at drawing.

I graduated from UW-Madison with a double major in geography and political science, a love for maps and no idea how to turn that into a job. After taking an internship with the National Park Service in Virginia, I learned how to use GIS while there and realized: this is really fun!

After GIS jobs in El Paso, TX and Janesville, WI sandwiched around returning to Madison to get my Graduate GIS Certificate, I am currently working in elections for the State of Wisconsin and in the GIS and Web Map Programming Master's program at UW-Madison.

Please see my resume for more information on my qualifications and feel free to reach out with questions with my contact info. Happy browsing!
